WHERE DID YOU GO

Our state does not like the Internet. It clogs it with spam, makes bot farms, tries to distort the information picture.

We launched this site to understand what is actually happening in the Russian-speaking segment of social networks. To do this, we made the ratings of posts and users. Here you will see the real life of Runet.

We do not create and do not moderate the posts that we show on the site, so the age limit is 18+.

HOW IT WORKS

We collect and rank the posts that have gained the most popularity in social networks in 30 minutes, 3 hours, day, week and month.

Determining the popularity of tweets, we look at the number of retweets and “I like” marks. Posts on instagram are rated by comments and likes. In the telegram, we track the number of absolute views of the record. At the same time, the post with the most views or likes will not necessarily be the most popular: the algorithm is able to take into account the influence of markups and other platform features.

Based on popular posts, we compile ratings of the most influential accounts in social networks.
